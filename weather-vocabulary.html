weather:region

Specifies area of the weather report which could be a state, city or area. E.g. NSW, Sydney, Canberra.

weather:date

Specifies the day of the forecast in a format of day DD month. E.g. Thursday 27 April

weather:maxTemp

Specifies the maximum temperature forecast for the day. The measurements are in degress celsius and is stored as a text value.

weather:minTemp

Specifies the minimum temperature forecast for the day. The measurements are in degress celsius and is stored as a text value.

weather:forecast

Describes the forecast for the day. Contains the weather for the day, the chance of rain and wind speeds. E.g. "Cloudy. Slight(20%) chance of a shower...Winds south to southwesterly 15 to 20km/h..."

weather:snowProbability

Specifies the probability of the snowfall being 1800m deep. Format will be text based and display a numerical value.

weather:condition

Specifies just the weather for the day. E.g. "Cloudy" or "Partly Cloudy".

weather:town

Refers to a certain area which could be a town or area. E.g. "Cabramurra", "Jindabyne", "Thredbo Top Station".
